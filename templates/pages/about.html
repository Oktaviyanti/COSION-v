About COSION

Computerized CAD System - Segmentation

COSION (Cancerous Object Segmentation) is a web-based application for automatic segmentation of suspected cancerous objects in ultrasound images. It uses the HERBAC (Hybrid Edge and Region Based Active Contour) method, developed by Nugroho et al. (2021).
