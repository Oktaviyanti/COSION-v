About COSION

Computerized CAD System - Segmentation

COSION (Cancerous Object Segmentation) is a web-based application developed by Anan Nugroho, et al (2022) who can segment objects suspected of cancer on ultrasound images automatically use a method called HERBAC (Hybrid Edge and Region Based Active Contour).
